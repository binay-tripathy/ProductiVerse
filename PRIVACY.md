# Privacy Policy for ProductiVerse

_Last updated: July 14, 2025_

## Overview

ProductiVerse is a Chrome extension designed to help users boost productivity through features such as a task manager, Pomodoro timer, ambient sounds, website blocker, and daily quotes. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

## Data Collection

- **No Personal Data Collected:**  
  ProductiVerse does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (such as name, email, address, age, or identification number).

- **No Sensitive Data Collected:**  
  ProductiVerse does **not** collect health, financial, authentication, personal communications, or location data.

## Local Storage

- **User Activity Stored Locally:**  
  ProductiVerse may store user activity data (such as tasks, timer settings, blocked sites, and ambient sound preferences) locally on your device. This data is used solely to provide extension functionality and improve your experience.

- **No Transmission to Third Parties:**  
  ProductiVerse does **not** transmit, sell, or share any user data to third parties.

## Data Usage

- All data stored by ProductiVerse is retained locally within your browser and is not accessible by the developer or any external service.
- Data is only used to enable core features of the extension (task management, timer, website blocking, etc.).

## Third-Party Services

- ProductiVerse does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, except for fetching daily quotes from public APIs. No personal data is sent during this process.

## User Control

- You may delete your local data at any time by removing the extension from your browser.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this file.

## Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please [open an issue](https://github.com/binay-tripathy/ProductiVerse/issues).
